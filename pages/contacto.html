Ubicados en el corazón de Ruzafa, Valencia

The trendy Ruzafa district is full of cafes, American-style bakeries, sidewalk cafes, bars and nightclubs, and restaurants serving burgers or Moroccan and Turkish specialties. The streets are lined with small art galleries, independent clothing stores and organic foods, while the stalls in the sprawling Mercado de Ruzafa are filled with produce. The Plaza de Toros hosts concerts and bullfights.
